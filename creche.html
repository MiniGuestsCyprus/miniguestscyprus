Mini Crèche for Weddings & Events

Luxury Mini Crèche and Nanny Service

Book our beautifully presented, all-in-one mini crèche for your wedding or event venue, featuring a wide range of high-quality toys, games & crafts.

A full nanny service with personal care is included as part of the package.

Key Details

  • ✓ Setup available in a safe & secluded area
  • ✓ Regular WhatsApp updates provided
  • ✓ Suitable for up to 3 children aged 2+*
  • ✓ Duration: 5–8 hours

Activities & Equipment

Every Mini Crèche booking is flexible and adaptable to ensure the fun doesn't stop for our mini guests.

A full Mini Crèche setup is provided, complete with a wide variety of toys, games, books & crafts suitable for different ages.

We’re always happy to tailor our toy and game selection. For more information, email miniguestscyprus@gmail.com.

Important Information

  • The Mini Crèche service will need to be set up in an air conditioned area due to the Cyprus heat.
  • The Mini Crèche service can be set up in a small room or a secluded area at the reception venue.
  • Families will be asked to provide a mini guests bag which must include: change of clothing, any required medications, sun cream, a light snack, comfort toy, sun hat, bottle of water, along with personal toiletries (nappies, baby wipes, etc.).
  • The mini guests bag is to keep the children protected from the sun and ensures the children will be safe from allergic reactions.
  • Booking 5–8 hours. Maximum 3 children. Additional children will require an additional nanny.
  • All our packages have flexibility, so if you have any personal enquiries contact us at miniguestscyprus@gmail.com.
  • We'll get in touch with you before the wedding or event to go over all the details to make sure everything is perfect for your mini guests.

*The Mini Crèche & Nanny service will be provided on a nanny/child ratio of 1:3.

If you require care for more children, an additional nanny can be arranged for a fee.

For mini guests aged 8 and above who are attending the event but do not require a nanny service, we can bring appropriate entertainment for them.

Contact us at miniguestscyprus@gmail.com for more details.
