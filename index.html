About Us

Hi! My name is Katie, founder and lead nanny at Mini Guests Cyprus.​

I'm a native English childcare professional with over 15 years of experience working with children of various ages in both the UK and Cyprus.​

My background includes teaching, nanny, and caring roles at nurseries, public and private primary schools, and special educational and behavioural needs (SEN) establishments.​

Through these experiences, I have developed key skills in delivering engaging activities suitable to each child’s individual needs, including:

  • Personal care
  • Feedings
  • Bathing
  • Administering medications
  • Supporting health requirements
  • Wheelchair use and hoisting
  • Bedtime routines

Since relocating to Cyprus in 2020, I’ve seen how happy children make for happy celebrations. I also recognise that couples hosting their dream wedding on this beautiful island need a professional they can rely on to ensure their mini guests are kept entertained in a safe and comfortable environment.​

That’s where we come in – a trusted & reliable childcare service for your VIP mini guests!​
