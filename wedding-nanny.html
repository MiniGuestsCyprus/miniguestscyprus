Wedding & Special Occasions Nanny

Tailored Wedding & Special Occasions Nanny Service

Book our personalised nanny service for your wedding or special event, uniquely tailored to suit the plans of couples & guests throughout the day.

Available for special occasions, celebrations, pre-wedding preparations (hair, make-up, photos), ceremony, photoshoots, reception, bedtime & night-time routine.

A full nanny service with personal care is included as part of the package, including dressing & feeding.

Key Details

  • ✓ Tailored to the plans of couples & guests
  • ✓ Mobile entertainment between venues with age-appropriate toys, activities, crafts & games
  • ✓ Regular WhatsApp updates provided
  • ✓ Suitable for up to 3 children aged 2+*
  • ✓ Duration: 5–8 hours

Activities & Equipment

Every Wedding & Special Occasions Nanny booking is flexible and adaptable to ensure the fun doesn't stop for our mini guests.

A fully stocked mobile entertainment kit is provided, which includes a varied selection of age-appropriate toys, games, books & crafts.

We’re always happy to tailor our toy and game selection and can include specific items from our equipment library to suit our mini guests' preferences.

Important Information

  • The Wedding & Special Occasions Nanny service will need to be set up in an air conditioned area due to the Cyprus heat.
  • The service can be set up in a small room, a secluded area at the reception venue, or on a child's table within the reception.
  • Families will be asked to provide a mini guests bag which must include: change of clothing, any required medications, sun cream, a light snack, comfort toy, sun hat, bottle of water, and personal toiletries (nappies, baby wipes, etc.).
  • All our packages have flexibility, so if you have any personal enquiries contact us at miniguestscyprus@gmail.com.
  • Booking 5–8 hours. Maximum 3 children. Additional children will require an additional nanny.
  • We'll get in touch with you before the wedding or event to go over all the details to make sure everything is perfect for your mini guests.

*The Wedding & Special Occasions Nanny service will be provided on a nanny/child ratio of 1:3.

If you require care for more children, an additional nanny can be arranged for a fee.

For mini guests aged 8 and above who are attending the event but do not require a nanny service, we can bring appropriate entertainment for them.

Contact us at miniguestscyprus@gmail.com for more details.
