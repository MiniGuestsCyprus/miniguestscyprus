Evening Nanny

Tailored Evening Nanny Service

Our Evening Nanny service is designed to support couples and guests who would like to relax and enjoy their celebrations while knowing their mini guests are safe, settled and cared for.

Available for evenings at hotels, villas and wedding venues, the service can include dinner time support, bedtime routines, storytelling and calm, age-appropriate entertainment until late.

Key Details

  • ✓ Special service for couples & guests requiring evening childcare support
  • ✓ Personal care, bedtime routines, storytelling & relaxing entertainment
  • ✓ Regular WhatsApp updates provided
  • ✓ Can be set up in a safe, secure location within your hotel or venue with in-room facilities
  • ✓ Suitable for up to 3 children aged 2+*
  • ✓ Duration: From 17:00 until 00:00 (flexible on request)

Activities & Evening Setup

Every Evening Nanny booking is planned around your child’s usual routine and preferences, to help them feel safe, relaxed and ready for a good night’s sleep.

A calm and comforting evening kit can be provided, including quiet activities and soothing resources to support bedtime and wind-down time.

We’re always happy to adapt the evening plan to include your child’s favourite toys, books or comfort items, and to follow specific routines you already use at home.

Important Information

  • The Evening Nanny service will need to be set up in a safe, secure indoor area with access to bathroom facilities.
  • The service can be provided in hotel rooms, villas or dedicated quiet areas within the wedding or event venue.
  • Families will be asked to provide a mini guests bag which may include: pyjamas, comfort toy, nappies/wipes (if needed), any required medications, and a change of clothes.
  • All our packages have flexibility, so if you have any personal enquiries contact us at miniguestscyprus@gmail.com.
  • Booking 5–7 hours as standard. Maximum 3 children. Additional children will require an additional nanny.
  • We'll get in touch with you before the wedding or event to go over all the details to make sure everything is perfect for your mini guests.

*The Evening Nanny service will be provided on a nanny/child ratio of 1:3.

If you require care for more children, an additional nanny can be arranged for a fee.

Contact us at miniguestscyprus@gmail.com for more details.
